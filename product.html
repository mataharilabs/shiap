Product Description

The Industrial Network Switch 24-Port Managed Gigabit is engineered for demanding enterprise environments. With 24 × 10/100/1000BASE-T RJ45 ports and 4 × SFP uplinks, this switch delivers line-rate performance for high-density networks.

Features Layer 2/3 management with VLAN, QoS, Link Aggregation, RSTP/MSTP support, and comprehensive SNMP v1/v2c/v3 management. Industrial-grade design rated for -40°C to +85°C operation, IP30 protection, and DIN-rail or rack mount options.

  • 24 × Gigabit RJ45 + 4 × SFP uplinks
  • Non-blocking 56Gbps switching capacity
  • Supports 802.1Q VLAN, 802.3ad LACP
  • Web-based + CLI management
  • FCC, CE, RoHS certified
